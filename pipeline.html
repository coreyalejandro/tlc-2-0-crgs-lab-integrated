Research → Paper → Product Pipeline

Steps 1–2: Bootstrapped working Steps 3–4: Partial Steps 5–6: In development

Every tier of TLC 2.0 — from a $100 hobbyist to a $1B Fortune 500 consortium — executes the same six-step constitutional pipeline. The pipeline is the same. The budget scales the depth of each step. No step may be skipped. Truth-state must be VERIFIED before proceeding to publication.

The pipeline terminates in a Tier-1 research-based consumer-facing product: the neurodivergent-first, accessible WebUI/TUI that is the direct output of constitutionally governed research — not a demo, not a placeholder, not a prototype. The thing the research was always building toward.

01

Hypothesis & Idea Generation

The AI analyzes current literature to find white spaces, contradictions, and unexplored anomalies. At Tier 1: open-access arXiv, PubMed, Semantic Scholar via sBERT similarity search. At Tier 5–6: cross-disciplinary knowledge graphs with real-time sensor feeds and physical lab integration.

The constitutional constraint: the hypothesis must be falsifiable. Pre-registration on OSF is required before data collection at Tier 3 and above. At Tier 1, a public GitHub hypothesis document with a hash-committed timestamp is sufficient.

02

Literature Synthesis & Verification

Full-text research retrieved and clustered by theme. Every assertion is cross-referenced with exact paragraph-level source data. At Tier 1: open-access only. At Tier 3+: Scopus, IEEE Xplore, Web of Science, ClinicalTrials.gov with paragraph-level matching via paywalled APIs.

The constitutional constraint: no citation may appear in a paper without a paragraph-level evidence anchor. The PROACTIVE agent enforces epistemic reliability features F1–F5 during literature synthesis — detecting cross-session claim persistence, artifact-claim divergence, post-hoc admission delta, and deference escalation.

03

Experimental Design & Execution

The AI generates methodology and code to run simulations or collect data. At Tier 1: FastAPI local server, SQLite, Python scripts, manual red-teaming checklist (30 prompts across 5 categories). At Tier 3: Docker Compose, PostgreSQL, automated CI/CD, chaos engineering. At Tier 5–6: digital twins, automated robotic laboratory instructions, physical liquid handlers, automated synthesis rigs.

The constitutional constraint: code must be executable. Documentation of intent is not equivalent to running code. The pre-commit Truth-State Gate blocks any claim of "working" without a passing test suite on file.

04

Adversarial Peer Review & Self-Critique

Before a draft is completed, an internal "Red Team" of specialized judge agents aggressively critiques the manuscript against the constitution. Reproducibility module verifies findings replicate within a predefined tolerance. The CHAE (Continuous Human Audit Engine) provides human oversight — volunteer audit buddy at Tier 1, part-time paid cell at Tier 2, 24/7 shifts at Tier 6.

The constitutional constraint: CCI (Constitutional Compliance Index) must remain above 0.85 at Tier 2, 0.90 at Tier 4, 0.95 at Tier 6. A single CHAE-issued technical stop order halts the pipeline until the invariant is restored.

05

Drafting, Formatting & IP Analysis

Verified findings formatted into publishing-ready templates. At Tier 1: Markdown or LaTeX. At Tier 3+: venue-specific LaTeX templates, automated reference management, BibTeX from Zotero group library. Novel claims mapped against patent databases to compile complete intellectual property applications and freedom-to-operate analyses.

Target venues: CHI, AAAI, NeurIPS, IEEE TSE, IJAIED, Nature Human Behaviour, FAccT, a "Neurodivergent-First AI at Scale" special issue, and a dedicated open-source tool paper at FOSS/ICSE.

06

Product Integration & Consumer Output

The terminal step. Verified research becomes a product. At Tier 1: concept feasibility report. At Tier 2: interactive web demo (Streamlit, Gradio). At Tier 3: working alpha prototype with design-for-manufacturing package. At Tier 5: physical prototype with regulatory documentation (IND, MSDS, CE marking). At Tier 6: market-ready consumer product at global scale with post-market AI surveillance feeding automated constitutional refinement loops.

The terminal product is the neurodivergent-first, accessible WebUI/TUI: a system that transforms complex AI research output into an interface usable by anyone — neurodivergent or neurotypical, on a $100 device or a $10,000 workstation. This is the Tier-1 research-based consumer-facing product the canonical mission describes. It is not aspirational. It is the architectural destination of every step above it.
