The Research Identity

Corey Alejandro is an AI Safety Research Engineer and Instructional Systems Researcher building Constitutional Runtime Governance Systems that govern the behavior of AI agents, learning systems, digital twins, and human-centered AI environments during execution.

The work sits at the intersection of:

  • Constitutional AI Safety — formally specified epistemic invariants enforced at runtime
  • Cognitive Diversity & Instructional Systems Design — neurodivergent-first co-design and governance
  • AI Engineering — production-grade, tested, deployable constitutional runtimes
  • Blockchain Runtime Governance — tamper-evident, independently auditable governance at scale

The tool that operationalizes all of the above is The Living Constitution 2.0 — a constitutional AI-governed, blockchain-runtime-verified R&D tool developed by Corey Alejandro.
