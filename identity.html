The Canonical Project Intent Statement

Canonical · Immutable · Version 1.0 · Ratified 2026-06-22
The project's objective is to develop a constitutional AI-governed, blockchain-runtime-verified research and development tool that will empower users across various resource categories — Fortune 500 enterprise corporations, academic research institutions, standard enterprise corporations, Tier-1 academic research institutions, small businesses and startups, and hobbyist developers — to produce Tier-1 research, resulting in Tier-1 research papers and, ultimately, a Tier-1 research-based consumer-facing product.

The project is designed to be accessible regardless of financial resources, allowing development with any amount from $100 to $1 billion.

What the Identity Commits To

Hybrid

The work crosses the boundary between research scientist and research engineer. The constitutional runtime is running infrastructure — not a framework, not a set of principles. The research produces papers. The engineering produces verified, tested, deployable systems. Both lanes are first-class. Neither is subordinate to the other.

AI Constitutional Runtime Governance Systems

This is the domain. Not "AI safety" in the generic sense. Constitutional Runtime Governance Systems: formal, evaluable specifications of epistemic invariants enforced during inference, research, and publication. The governance is structural, not advisory. The constitution executes. The runtime enforces.

Research Scientist AND Research Engineer

Both titles simultaneously. The AND is not a conjunction — it is the core claim. The same person who designs the constitutional invariants implements the runtime that enforces them. The same person who conducts the empirical study writes the test suite that validates it. This duality is the unique capability TLC 2.0 was built to demonstrate.

One-of-a-Kind, State-of-the-Art R&D Development Practice

There is no other researcher-engineer who has built a constitutional runtime, derived constitutions from domain theory, and simultaneously conducted Tier-1 empirical research governed by that runtime. This is not a claim of superiority over a field. It is a description of a specific, novel capability that does not yet have a category.

  • Describing the work as "just a portfolio" or "just a framework"
  • Describing Corey as "an AI safety researcher" without the constitutional runtime governance specification
  • Omitting "Hybrid" — the hybrid scientist/engineer duality is the identity, not a qualifier
