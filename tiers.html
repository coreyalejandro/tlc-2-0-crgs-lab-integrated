Six Tiers. $100 to $1 Billion.

Tier 1–2: Working / Partial Tier 3–4: In Development Tier 5–6: Planned

The canonical mission of TLC 2.0 is to empower every resource category — from a hobbyist developer with $100 to a Fortune 500 global consortium with $1 billion — to produce Tier-1 research. The same constitutional protocol governs every tier. Budget determines scale, tooling, and governance depth — not scientific validity.

Every tier below carries its truth_status: what is verified working today vs. what is roadmapped. No tier overstates its current capability.

The Constant Across All Tiers

Budget determines scale and tooling. It does not determine scientific validity. Every tier executes the same constitutional research protocol:

  • Every claim carries a truth_status — verified, partial, draft, unverified, or planned
  • Every claim is evidence-bound — no claim exists without an evidence artifact
  • Every claim is inspectable — the evidence can be independently reviewed
  • Every governance decision is logged — append-only, tamper-resistant at minimum, blockchain-verified at scale
  • Neurodivergent-first design is non-negotiable at every tier — the governance system was designed for a monotropic, ADHD+OCD+autistic operator and that design is a feature, not an accommodation
